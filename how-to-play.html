How to Play?

ARE YOU READY? CHOOSE A FRIEND AND FIGURE IF THEY COULD BE YOUR BEST FRIEND.


To start the game click on one of the six icons with your chosen friend's name on the main page.
You will be asked 10 questions and for each correct answer you will receive a point.
At the end of the quiz you will get the result so you can see whether you know that friend well enough to be your best friend.

Have Fun!
