How to Play?

Are you one of the oldest FRIENDS?
Test you memory and see if you're the most loyal friend of all!


To start the game...

You know the lyrics of Smelly Cat by heart? You are sure of Rachel's childhood bedroom colour? If you feel like the seventh friend this test is for you! You will be asked a series of questions and for each correct answer you will receive X point.
At the end of the quiz you will get your result and find out if you would get a spot at the reserved couch at Central Perk.

Game on!
